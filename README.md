# AllScale Lab

AllScale Lab is the experimentation arm of [AllScale](https://allscale.io), dedicated to leveraging stablecoin infrastructure for innovative projects that generate positive externality for the world.

## Mission

We believe stablecoin rails can do more than move money. AllScale Lab explores how stable, programmable digital currencies can be applied to solve real-world problems — from financial inclusion and transparent aid distribution to sustainable commerce and public goods funding.

## What We Do

- **Experiment** — Prototype novel applications of stablecoin technology beyond traditional finance
- **Measure Impact** — Quantify the positive externalities our projects create for communities and ecosystems
- **Open Source** — Share findings, tools, and frameworks so others can build on our work
- **Collaborate** — Partner with researchers, NGOs, and builders who share our vision

## Focus Areas

- **Financial Inclusion** — Reducing barriers to cross-border economic participation for underserved populations
- **Transparent Public Goods** — On-chain mechanisms for funding and verifying public goods delivery
- **Sustainable Commerce** — Stablecoin-powered incentive structures that reward positive environmental and social outcomes
- **Programmable Impact** — Smart contract systems that automate and enforce impact-aligned financial flows

## About AllScale

AllScale is a next-generation SaaS platform that removes friction from cross-border finance using stablecoins. AllScale Lab operates as its dedicated research and experimentation division, pushing the boundaries of what stablecoin infrastructure can achieve for the public good.
